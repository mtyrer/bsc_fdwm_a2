Fun with Smiggy

The concepts of earning and spending money are becoming more and more abstract for children. Today most payments are done digitally; no physical currency is exchanged.

The old educational concept of a piggy bank to save recurring allowances and one-off payments for special events, good behaviour or completion of chores is difficult to follow for both parents and children if this cash concept is not mirrored in daily life. The key to good money habits are patients, planning, values and making good choices. The ability to do this relies on setting solid foundations through education. Smiggy and friends will give you the opportunity to translate these concepts in a fun way to your children. Along with Smiggy, our smart piggy bank, we have many friends to help instil various messages. There is Sally “Savings” Squirrel, Mr Mole and his goals, Oscar the wise owl and many more.
